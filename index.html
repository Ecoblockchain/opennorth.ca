Mission and Team

Open North is a Canadian nonprofit that creates online tools to educate and empower citizens to participate actively in Canadian democracy.

Improve access to government information

Availability

Pulling it out of dark corners of government websites, taking it out of PDFs and making access to information requests

Accessibility

Providing context to numbers, explaining legislative jargon and using language everyone understands

Relevance

Letting citizens find information specific to their community, interests and situation

Make participation easy, fun, and meaningful

Design

Work with governments to design new participatory processes and to ensure citizen’s voices are heard

Engage

Increase the number of ways citizens can engage with elected officials and government information

Collaboration

Help citizens connect with each other and organize around issues they care about
