Data Standards Development

We design data models and data formats used by governments, nonprofits and businesses to make civic data useful and interoperable, including Popolo for legislative data and Open511 for road events. We co-lead the standards stream of the Open Government Partnership's Open Data Working Group. Our work on other standards includes Open Contracting and W3C linked data vocabularies.

Government
Open Data

Open North helps governments develop powerful and accessible open data portals. Open North is an expert in implementing and customizing CKAN, the open source leader for open data portals, to bring high quality solutions that fit the budgets of most municipalities. Open North helps governments design publication processes and best practices and provides training to get the most out of open data initiatives.

Legislative
Monitoring

Our online tools make it easier for citizens to exercise their political influence and participate actively in decision-making processes. In Canada, we partnered with the Springtide Collective to create OpenHouseNS, and we support OpenParliament. Internationally, Represent Boundaries, our Poplus component, is used by some of the world's most popular democratic websites.
